Privacy Policy

Ammo Vault is designed with privacy as a core principle. Here is exactly what the app does and does not do with your data:

  • All data is stored exclusively on your device, encrypted with AES-256-GCM
  • No data is ever transmitted to any server, cloud service, or third party
  • No user accounts are created or required
  • No analytics, telemetry, tracking, or advertising of any kind
  • No personal information is collected — not your name, email, location, or device ID
  • Face ID / Touch ID authentication is handled entirely by iOS through the Secure Enclave
  • Deleting the app permanently and irrecoverably deletes all stored data
  • The app requires no internet connection and functions entirely offline

This policy is effective as of March 2026. If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted on this page.

Support

Having an issue or want to request a feature? Here is how to get help:

  • Email: support@ammovaultapp.com
  • Feature requests and bug reports are welcome and actively reviewed

Can I back up my data?

Not yet. Data export and import is planned for a future update.

What if I forget my passphrase?

There is no recovery mechanism. Without your passphrase or biometric unlock, the vault cannot be decrypted. This is by design for your security.

Does the app work without internet?

Yes. Ammo Vault is fully offline. No internet connection is ever needed or used.

Can I add custom calibers?

Not yet. The app supports 38 built-in calibers covering the most popular US rounds. Custom caliber support is on the roadmap.

Is my data shared with anyone?

No. Your data never leaves your device. There are no servers, no cloud sync, and no third-party services of any kind.
