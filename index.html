Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Purpose-built for shooters who want a fast, private way to track what they have on hand.

🎯

Ammunition Tracking

Log rounds by caliber, grain weight, and factory/reload status. Quick +/− buttons for single rounds, or tap the count to enter bulk amounts. Notes field on every entry for brand, lot number, or load data.

⚙

Reloading Supplies

Track primers, powder, brass, and bullets separately. Categorize by caliber or primer size. Perfect for reloaders managing components across multiple cartridges.

🔫

Range Day Mode

Dedicated screen to log an entire shooting session. Enter rounds fired per ammo type across all calibers, review the total, and submit — one tap logs everything and updates your inventory.

🔔

Low Stock Alerts

Set custom thresholds for any caliber or reloading supply. Get a local notification when inventory drops below your limit. Never show up to the range short again.

🔒

Face ID & Touch ID

Unlock your vault with biometrics. The encryption key lives in the iOS Secure Enclave behind biometric protection — your passphrase is never stored on disk.

📋

Activity Log

Full audit trail of every add, subtract, delete, range day, and supply change with timestamps. See exactly what changed and when.

Security

Military-grade
encryption.

Your inventory data is encrypted with AES-256-GCM via Apple's CryptoKit framework — the same standard used to protect classified government information. Every byte is encrypted before it touches disk.

Your passphrase is salted with 256 random bits stored in the iOS Keychain, then derived through SHA-256 to produce the encryption key. No data ever leaves your device. No accounts. No cloud. No telemetry.

Privacy Policy

Your data stays yours.

Effective Date: March 2026

Data Collection

Ammo Vault collects no personal data whatsoever. The app does not require an account, does not connect to the internet, and does not transmit any data to any server, third party, or analytics service.

Data Storage

All inventory data is stored exclusively on your device in an AES-256-GCM encrypted file. The encryption key is derived from your passphrase using a unique 256-bit salt stored in the iOS Keychain. No data is stored in iCloud, on remote servers, or anywhere outside your device.

Biometric Data

When Face ID or Touch ID is enabled, the app uses Apple's LocalAuthentication framework to authenticate access to the derived encryption key stored in the iOS Keychain. Ammo Vault never accesses, processes, or stores biometric data directly. All biometric operations are handled entirely by the iOS operating system.

Third-Party Services

Ammo Vault uses no third-party SDKs, analytics tools, crash reporters, ad networks, or tracking services of any kind.

Notifications

The app may request permission to send local notifications for low-stock alerts. These notifications are generated and delivered entirely on-device. No notification data is transmitted externally.

Data Deletion

Deleting the app permanently removes all stored data, including the encrypted vault file and all Keychain entries. There is no backup or recovery mechanism.

Children's Privacy

Ammo Vault is not directed at children under 17 and does not knowingly collect information from minors.
